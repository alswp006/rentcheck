## Summary
The codebase has a clear domain-driven structure with solid simulation logic and typed storage contracts, but suffers from fragmented utility organization (parallel flat files alongside subdirectories), several pieces of dead code, and repeated patterns (isTossApp detection, toast state) that should be extracted. The core simulation and state layers are sound, but the peripheral infrastructure needs consolidation.
