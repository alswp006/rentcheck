Generated: 2026-03-28T16:58:28.208Z

## Summary
The codebase has a well-typed domain layer (types.ts, engine.ts) and clear routing intentions, but suffers from several structural problems: orphaned files, a duplicated environment-detection pattern across three hooks, a shadowed storage module, and a dead generic utility layer that the specific storage modules bypass entirely. The core simulation logic appears sound but the surrounding plumbing needs cleanup.
